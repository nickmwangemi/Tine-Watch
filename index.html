About Us




About Us

Tine Watch Security Systems Ltd is a duly registered business under the Laws of Kenya. We have expansive experience in the global security and investigation industry which makes us the best bet for our clients' needs, to protect their assets and premises.

We have well trained and motivated staff who are well equipped and always ready to act when need arises. We focus our efforts in constant improvement of the quality and standards of our operations through having utmost attention, care, cooperation and punctual execution.

We also focus our efforts in constant improvement of quality standards and in reinvestment in business development and training. We strive to reach the highest standards of honest and ethical behaviour by promoting integrity and good governance.

The Company maintains a reasonable public liability/professional indemnity policy covering the customer against any potential loss or costs incurred as a result of any willful negligence on the part of our employees. Tine Watch is mindful of the fact that they are not an insurance Company but a security company that minimizes risks to its customers.

Services

Guarding Services

We offer professional security presence to keep you safe for the dual purpose of guarding against those who intend to trespass your premises and controlling physical access to your premises. The service includes security guards ,alarm response, dogs and CCTV cameras.

Temporary Security Services

We offer security services to events including parties, political rallies, private meetings, and inauguraurations.

Security Equipment Installation

We install security equipment which is complementary to total security provision .This includes, but limited to CCTVs, electric fences, access control systems, sensor and detectors, fire detection systems, alarm response system and much more.

Investigation Devices

We offer expertised investgations by working closely with the client to understand investigation objectives and coordinate our approach to utilize the appopriate resources.

VIP Protection Services

Dog Services

Private Investigation

We offer leading private investigation services based on our expertise in the field and ensure integrity, discretion and professionalism while at it.
